Venice

Venice, the floating city of Italy, is a mesmerizing labyrinth of canals, bridges, and historic architecture. Lose yourself in the narrow alleyways, explore iconic landmarks like St. Mark's Basilica and the Grand Canal, and savor authentic Italian cuisine in charming trattorias.

Price: €499

Paris

Paris, the City of Light, beckons with its romantic ambiance, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture. Admire the Eiffel Tower's majestic silhouette, wander through art-filled museums like the Louvre, and indulge in delicious pastries at quaint sidewalk cafes. Explore the opulent Palace of Versailles.

Price: €679

Santorini

Santorini, a captivating Greek island in the Aegean Sea, boasts stunning sunsets, whitewashed buildings, and crystal-clear waters. Explore picturesque villages perched atop volcanic cliffs, unwind on black sand beaches, and savor local delicacies amidst breathtaking scenery.

Price: €899
